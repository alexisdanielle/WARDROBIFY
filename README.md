
# WARDROBIFY 

A student's dilemma is what to wear every day in the midst of busy midterms and finals. Wardrobify is an AI Stylist that helps you scan and classify your wardrobe. Don't know what to wear? Wardrobify is here for you! 

Features 
1. Weather Checker -  make sure outfit matches the weather outside
2. Closet Scanner - scan items in your wardrobe and it automatically classifies!
3. AI Stylist - tell me the vibe and we'll style you! 
4. Wishlist - checks to see if what you want to buy has duplicates or is a new find!
5. Weekly Prep - designs a week of outfits for you during busy times like finals
